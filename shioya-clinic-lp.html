Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Is VIP service available?

+

Yes, we offer VIP services including completely private room examinations, after-hours services, and dedicated doctors and nurses. Your privacy and comfort are our top priorities. Please let us know your requirements in advance.

Q. Do you accommodate religious considerations and dietary restrictions?

+

We accommodate all dietary restrictions including Halal, vegetarian, vegan, and allergy requirements. We also provide appropriate accommodations for prayer times and other religious considerations when notified in advance.

Q. Is a medical visa required?

+

For health screenings only, a short-term stay visa is sufficient. However, if treatment is required, a medical visa may be necessary. Our partner guarantor institutions provide visa acquisition support.

Q. When will I receive my test results?

+

Basic test results are provided on the same day. Detailed pathology results are sent as translated reports in your native language within 1-2 weeks. Online result consultations are also available.

Q. What payment methods are available?

+

We accept cash, credit cards (VISA, MasterCard, AMEX), bank transfers, and cryptocurrency payments. Please consult us about international insurance coverage.
